Getting Started

1

Import Your Credit Report

Your first step to using Creditup is to import your credit report. Your credit report will be automatically analyzed to look for negative items.

2

Save Your Name, Address, etc.

This information is used to automatically populate dispute letter templates with your personal information. Without it, you will need to manually edit each letter.

3

Create Dispute Letters

Automatically generate dispute letters to send to credit bureaus for any negative items found in your report.

4

Track Your Disputes

Stay updated on the status of your disputes by tracking their progress in real-time.
